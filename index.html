The Pets

Dog

Faithful and energetic, dogs make the perfect companion for any adventure.

Cat

Independent yet affectionate, cats bring a calm presence to any home.

Pig

Highly intelligent and social creatures that enjoy being part of a group.

Rabbit

Quiet and gentle pets that love fresh greens and plenty of space to hop.

Parrot

Colorful and vocal, these birds are known for their amazing ability to mimic sounds.
